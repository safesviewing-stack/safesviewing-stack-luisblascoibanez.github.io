About

I don’t design objects. I design systems, processes and experiences.

I am an industrial designer based in Valencia with a strong technical background and a clear interest in the space where engineering and design intersect.

My work lives between logic, structure and feasibility, and aesthetics, use and experience. Before focusing on design, I trained in electricity, completing intermediate and advanced technical degrees.

I enjoy transforming complex problems into clear, functional and well-resolved solutions. For me, design is balance: between precision and intuition, technology and sensitivity.
