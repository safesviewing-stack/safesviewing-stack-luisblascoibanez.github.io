About

I don’t design objects. I design systems, processes and experiences.

I am an industrial designer based in Valencia with a strong technical background and a clear interest in the space where engineering and design intersect.

My work lives between logic and sensitivity, structure and experience. Before focusing on design, I trained in electricity, gaining a deep understanding of systems, materials and manufacturing processes.
